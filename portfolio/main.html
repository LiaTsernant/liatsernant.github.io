ABOUT ME

A full stack software engineer: very eager to learn, with particular excitement for the server-side. Extensive past experience of working in teams & on tight schedules from previous music career.

My previous experience in music allowed me to learn how to respect my colleagues’ ideas, to work with stress, to always pay attention, and to keep learning from more experienced people.
I learned how to communicate with people, how to present my thoughts and deal with critics.

After 3 years of being a member of and leading in various orchestras, I found my interest in Software Engineering, because I am fascinated with how big companies such as Facebook, Google, and Apple improve people’s quality of life. I would love to create products that benefit the lives of everyday people.

Skill Set: CS skills: JavaScript, Ruby, Python, React, JQuery, AJAX, Node/Express, HTML, CSS, Git, Problem Solving. Languages: Russian, English, French.
